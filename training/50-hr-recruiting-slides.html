31 / 50
Performance & Development

Goal Setting Framework

We use OKRs at the company level and individual level. Goals cascade from company → team → individual.

Good Goal Example
Objective: Improve onboarding experience
KR1: Reduce time-to-first-value from 8 days to 3 days
KR2: Achieve 90% onboarding completion rate
KR3: New hire NPS > 70
Weak Goal Example
Objective: Make onboarding better
KR1: Improve the onboarding deck
KR2: Get more feedback
KR3: Be more responsive
Not measurable, not time-bound, not outcome-oriented.

Performance & Development

Feedback Model

SBI
Situation → Behavior → Impact
"In yesterday's standup (S), when you walked through the blockers with clear next steps (B), the team was able to unblock in 30 minutes instead of a day (I)."
Continuous
Not just at review time
Give feedback within 48 hours. Regular 1:1s are the primary feedback channel. Don't save it for the cycle.
Two-Way
Managers receive feedback too
Skip-level meetings quarterly. Anonymous upward feedback in engagement surveys. Manager effectiveness score.

Total Rewards

Comp Philosophy

We pay competitively to attract and retain top talent. Compensation reflects role level, market data, and individual performance — not negotiation skill.

Benchmarking

Radford / Pave data. Updated annually. Target: 75th percentile for base, 65th for total comp.

Pay Equity

Annual pay equity audit across gender, ethnicity, and tenure. Adjustments made proactively.

Transparency

Comp bands published internally for every level. Managers trained on comp conversations.
