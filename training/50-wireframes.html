Design & UX

Design Principles

01Clarity over clevernessEvery screen should be instantly understandable. If it needs explanation, simplify.
02Speed is a featureReduce friction at every step. Fewer taps, faster feedback, zero dead ends.
03Trust through transparencyShow the math, the timeline, and the status. Never leave users guessing.
04Design for contextOur users are multitasking, on mobile, often in a hurry. Design for their reality.
05Accessible by defaultEvery interaction must work for every user, regardless of ability or device.
